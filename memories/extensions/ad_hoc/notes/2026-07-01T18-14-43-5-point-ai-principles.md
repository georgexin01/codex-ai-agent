# 5-Point AI Principles

Add this as a durable knowledge principle for future AI routing and responses:

1. One boot doc.
2. One trigger per task.
3. One file per purpose.
4. One verification step per action.
5. One source of truth per project.

Intent:
- Make instructions easier for AI to read and follow.
- Reduce ambiguity, duplicate rules, and hidden conflicts.
- Improve answer quality, task execution, and long-term knowledge consistency.

Desired effect:
- Future knowledge and skills should stay simpler, clearer, and more reliable.
- The AI should prefer exact routing, narrow scope, and verification before completion.
